About Kit 22/23 23/34

Introducing the brand-new football kits for the "Wild Irtysh" team, a symbol of pride and unity for fans and players alike. These kits encapsulate the spirit of the team while embracing the rich traditions and legacy of the sport.

Home Kit: Pure Elegance in White

The "Wild Irtysh" home kit is a striking embodiment of pure elegance and class. Crafted in pristine white, it reflects the team's commitment to excellence and sportsmanship. The jersey features a modern and sleek design that seamlessly blends tradition with innovation. The body of the shirt is predominantly white, with subtle design elements that add depth and character.


At the heart of the jersey is the team's logo, proudly displayed over the chest in vibrant blue and green, which pays homage to the club's roots and natural surroundings. The jersey's breathable fabric ensures optimal comfort for players, allowing them to perform at their best while representing their beloved team.

Away Kit: Bold and Dominant in Blue

For those intense away games, the "Wild Irtysh" team dons their striking blue away kit. This kit exudes confidence, strength, and a fearless spirit. The jersey is predominantly deep blue, reminiscent of the wide Irtysh River and the vast skies that grace the team's hometown.

Whether in the dazzling white of the home kit or the dominant blue of the away kit, these jerseys represent the passion and dedication that define this remarkable football club.

"The Wild Irtysh team's new football kits are more than just uniforms; they are symbols of identity, pride, and the unwavering support of fans."Asainov Damir, designer and choach assistent.
